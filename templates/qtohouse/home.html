How it works

Our certified take-off specialists use a structured process to accurately calculate the quantities of various construction materials required for each project. Here's a brief overview of how our construction take-off services work:

  • Strategic Analysis of Project Plans:

    Our Quantity take-off estimators first review all relevant plans and drawings for the project, including civil plans, architectural plans, structural plans and specifications. We review the takeoff scope as detail by our customers and check the plans for that scope.

  • Streamlined Quantity Take-Off:

    Our material takeoff specialists load the PDF plans into our take-off software such as planswift or bluebeam. Drawings are scaled and the quantity take-off is performed. It involves measuring, counting and quantifying various materials required for the construction project. Material such as concrete, masonry, structural steel, lumber framing, roofing, siding painting, flooring etc, are taken off one by one.

  • Project Cost Overview:

    We put all the materials and their quantities as per their CSI trades into the excel spreadsheet. Stable wastage factors and considered for the materials and the final quantities are listed with appropriate unit of measures. We also price the material, labour and equipment costs if required by our customer.

  • Construction Take-Off Deliverables:

    Our final construction take-off deliverables include material estimate in excel, color-coded markups in PDF and notes/RFIS related to the project. Our client gets full ownership of all deliverables. The files can edit, modified or shared as needed by the customer.

    Our take-off estimating services have been utilized in a variety of mega construction projects, From commercial buildings to residential apartments and public infrastructure projects. For Example, Our take-off services were used during the construction of the Hudson yards development in New York city to manage the projects cost management requirements.
